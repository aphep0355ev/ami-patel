About

profile

I work on non-perturbative dark matter phenomenology — how quantum scattering translates into observable cosmological and astrophysical signatures.

My research builds frameworks connecting scattering amplitudes, Sommerfeld enhancement, and coupled-channel dynamics, with a focus on threshold effects and resonance structures. I'm drawn to problems where taking quantum mechanics seriously — rather than approximating it away — changes what we predict.

Currently based at the University of Warsaw, working with Dr. Ayuki Kamada, with an ongoing collaboration with Kavli IPMU in Japan.
